TR-181 Issue 2 Amendment 15 has no impact on Energy Efficiency.

3.2 IPv6

TR-181 Issue 2 Amendment 15 defines IPv6 extensions (introduced in Issue 2 Amendment 2) to the Device:2 data model.

3.3 Security

TR-181 Issue 2 Amendment 15 has no impact on Security.

TR-181 Issue 2 Amendment 15 has no impact on Privacy.
